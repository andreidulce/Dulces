Beats

Beats Electronics (formerly Beats by Dr. Dre, or simply Beats by Dre) is a subsidiary of Apple Inc. that produces audio products. Headquartered in Culver City, California, U.S. the company was founded by music producer and rapper Dr. Dre and Interscope Records co-founder Jimmy Iovine.
